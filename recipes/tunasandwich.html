Tuna Sandwich


Tuna Sandwiches, what more can be said?

Ingredients

  • Can of tuna, ideally in brine
  • Fresh bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Arugula/rocket

Directions

  1. Mix 1 tbsp mayonaise with a can of tuna in a bowl, season to taste
  2. Cut 2 slices of bread
  3. Put a bed of arugula/rocket on your bread, no need to butter this with the amount of mayo in it
  4. Slice your cherry tomatoes, into either halves or quarters
  5. Use a spoon to apply your tuna mayo on top of your arugula/rocket
  6. Scatter your cherry tomatoes on top of the tuna
  7. ... add the top of your bread
  8. Optional: Slice into triangles
  9. Enjoy!
