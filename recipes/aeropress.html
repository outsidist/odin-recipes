Aeropress: How To
(JH Method)


The Aeropress, not a normal recipe but one worth learning

Ingredients

  • At least 13g coffee
  • Filtered water, 80-90Â°c
  • Sugar (never)
  • Milk (rarely)

Directions

  1. Add a filter to your aeropress cap, there is no need to wet it first
  2. Screw the cap onto the bottom of the aeropress and place on top of your cup
  3. Weigh out 13-18g of coffee into the chamber
  4. Pour in around 50g of water, wetting as much off of the ground coffee as possible
  5. Wait 15-20 seconds, this is known as the bloom
  6. Pour in 200 ml of water
  7. Put the plunger into the top of the chamber and wait
  8. Wait a little longer, maybe give it a little swirl to break up any bed that might form
  9. Wait a little longer still... 2 minutes has now elapsed
  10. Plunge! Plunging should take around 30 seconds
  11. Shake it off!
  12. Enjoy!
